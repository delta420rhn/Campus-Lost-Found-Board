# User Personas

## Persona 1: Amir – The Stressed Student Who Lost Something

**Age:** 20  
**Year:** 2nd year  
**Faculty:** Engineering  
**Device:** Mostly uses his phone

**Background:**  
Amir is a busy student who spends most of his time moving between lectures, labs, and the library. He is not very tech-savvy but he uses his phone constantly for everything. Last month he lost his student ID card and had to spend money replacing it because he had no way to find out if anyone picked it up.

**Goals:**
- Quickly report a lost item and get it back
- Not waste time searching through multiple WhatsApp groups
- Be notified if someone finds what he lost

**Frustrations:**
- Social media posts get ignored
- Going to the security office is inconvenient
- He doesn't know who to contact when something goes missing

**How he uses the platform:**  
He opens the site on his phone, posts a lost item with a photo and description, and checks back later to see if anyone responded.

## Persona 2: Fatima – The Responsible Finder

**Age:** 22  
**Year:** 4th year  
**Faculty:** Business  
**Device:** Laptop and phone

**Background:**  
Fatima is organized and responsible. She found a wallet in the cafeteria last semester and felt bad when she couldn't figure out who to return it to. She gave it to a staff member at the counter but never found out if it was returned.

**Goals:**
- Post a found item so the owner can see it and claim it
- Not be stuck holding onto someone else's belongings
- Contact the owner without giving out her own number

**Frustrations:**
- No clear system to return found items
- Handing it to random staff feels unreliable
- She doesn't want to post her phone number on a public group

**How she uses the platform:**  
She takes a photo of the item, posts it as a Found listing with the location, and waits for the owner to message her through the platform.
