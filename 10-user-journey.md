# User Journey

## Journey 1: Student Reports a Lost Item

**User:** Amir (lost his keys near the gym)

| Step | Action | Emotion |
|------|--------|---------|
| 1 | Realizes his keys are missing after leaving the gym | Stressed, worried |
| 2 | Opens the Campus Lost & Found website on his phone | Hopeful |
| 3 | Clicks "Post a Listing" and selects "Lost" | Neutral |
| 4 | Fills in item name (keys), description, category, location (gym), uploads a photo | Focused |
| 5 | Submits the listing | Slightly relieved |
| 6 | Receives a message from a student who found the keys | Very relieved |
| 7 | Meets the finder on campus and gets keys back | Happy |
| 8 | Marks the listing as resolved | Satisfied |

---

## Journey 2: Student Posts a Found Item

**User:** Fatima (found a wallet in the cafeteria)

| Step | Action | Emotion |
|------|--------|---------|
| 1 | Finds a wallet on a table in the cafeteria | Concerned, wants to help |
| 2 | Opens the Campus Lost & Found website | Determined |
| 3 | Clicks "Post a Listing" and selects "Found" | Neutral |
| 4 | Takes a photo of the wallet, fills in description and location | Careful |
| 5 | Submits the listing | Relieved that she did something useful |
| 6 | Owner messages her through the platform | Happy |
| 7 | Owner comes to cafeteria to collect the wallet | Satisfied |

---

## Journey 3: Student Searches for a Lost Item

**User:** A student looking for a lost phone

| Step | Action | Emotion |
|------|--------|---------|
| 1 | Loses phone and checks the Lost & Found board | Anxious |
| 2 | Searches for "phone" and filters by building/area | Hopeful |
| 3 | Finds a matching Found listing | Excited |
| 4 | Sends a message to the poster | Relieved |
| 5 | Confirms details and collects the phone | Very happy |
