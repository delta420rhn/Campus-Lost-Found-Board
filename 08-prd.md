# Product Requirement Document (PRD)

## Product Name
Campus Lost & Found Board

## Purpose
To give university students a simple and centralized platform to post, search, and recover lost items on campus.

## Target Users
University students who have lost items or found items on campus and want a reliable way to connect with each other.

## Core Features

### 1. User Authentication
Students can register an account using their university email and log in securely. Only registered users can post listings or contact other users.

### 2. Post a Listing
A user can create a new listing and choose whether it is a "Lost" or "Found" post. They fill in the item name, description, category, campus location where the item was lost or found, and optionally upload a photo.

### 3. Browse Listings
All active listings are shown on the main board. Users can scroll through them or search by keyword. Listings show the item name, photo, category, location, and date posted.

### 4. Search and Filter
Users can search by keyword and filter results by category (e.g., electronics, clothing, keys) and by campus location.

### 5. Contact Poster
A user who recognizes an item can send a message to the poster through an in-app contact form. The poster's personal contact details are not shown publicly.

### 6. Mark as Resolved
Once an item is returned, the original poster can mark the listing as resolved. Resolved listings are hidden from the main board.

## Success Metrics
- Students are able to post a listing in under 2 minutes
- Students can find relevant listings through search without scrolling the whole board
- Items are returned through the platform at a higher rate than through social media groups

## Assumptions
- Students have university email addresses that can be used for sign-up verification
- Most students access the platform from a mobile browser
- There is no need for payment or shipping; all item pickups are done in person on campus
