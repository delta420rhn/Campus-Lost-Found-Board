# Project Overview

## Project Name
Campus Lost & Found Board

## Project Summary
Campus Lost & Found Board is a simple web platform for university students to report and recover lost items on campus. Students can post a lost item or a found item with a photo, short description, and the campus location. Other students can browse the board and contact the poster directly if they recognize their belongings.

## Goals
The main goal is to make it easier for students to recover their lost belongings without relying on notice boards or word of mouth. The app gives every student one central place to check when they lose something or when they find something that belongs to someone else.

## Scope
The project covers the following:
- Student registration and login
- Posting lost or found items with photo, description, and location
- Browsing and searching all active listings
- Contacting the poster to claim an item
- Marking an item as resolved once returned

Out of scope for this version: payment, item shipping, admin moderation panel, mobile app.

## Tech Stack (Planned)
- Frontend: HTML, CSS, JavaScript (or React)
- Backend: Node.js with Express (or Django)
- Database: MySQL or PostgreSQL
- Hosting: To be decided
