# Problem Statement

## The Problem
Every semester, students on university campuses lose personal items like phones, wallets, keys, ID cards, and bags. When this happens, there is no easy or reliable way to find them. Most campuses have physical lost and found boxes managed by security or admin offices, but many students don't even know where these are or forget to check. Items that are found by other students often end up unclaimed because the finder has no way to reach the owner.

Social media groups exist, but posts get buried quickly and not every student is in every group. There is no single organized place where students can go to post or search for lost and found items.

## Impact
- Students lose valuable belongings permanently even when someone on campus found them
- Finders are left with items they don't know what to do with
- The physical lost and found offices are inconvenient and often unstaffed or ignored
- Time is wasted as students search through multiple platforms with no result

## Proposed Solution
A dedicated web platform for the campus community where students can post lost or found items in a structured way. Each listing includes a photo, description, and campus location. Students can search listings and directly contact whoever posted. Once the item is returned, the listing can be marked as resolved and removed from active view.

This removes the friction on both sides and gives the campus community one reliable place to handle lost and found items.
